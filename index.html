INTERESTS
We focus on:

                  1. Epigenetic regulation during the early development of animal embryos;

                  2. Epigenetic regulation during stem cell differentiation;

                  3. the function of regulatory sequences such as promoters, enhancers, insulators, and three-dimensional genomes
                  in development and cell fate determination;

                  4. Regulation mechanism of epigenetic related human diseases.
